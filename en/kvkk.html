Personal Data Protection Policy

As Enmot Industry, we show maximum sensitivity to the issue of the security of your personal data. Your personal data are processed and stored in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Law No.6698 ("KVKK").


Disclosure as Data Controller

As enmot.com, whose detailed corporate information is published below, in accordance with KVKK numbered 6698 and in the capacity of data controller, your personal data, within the framework explained on this page, will be recorded, stored, updated and disclosed/transferred to the third parties in cases permitted by the legislation, will be classified and processed in the ways listed in the KVKK.


In What Way Your Personal Data Can Be Processed

Pursuant to the KVKK numbered 6698, the personal data you have shared with Enmot Industry can be processed by us in whole or in part, automatically or non-automatic means, provided that it is part of any data recording system, by means of being acquired, saved, stored, changed, rearranged, in short, as the subject of any transaction performed on data. Within the scope of KVKK, any transaction performed on the data is accepted as "processing of personal data".


Storage and Protection of Personal Data

Enmot Endüstri is obliged to protect the systems and databases in which your personal data is stored, to prevent the unlawful processing of personal data in accordance with Article 12 of the KVKK and to prevent unauthorized access, to take software measures and physical security measures such as hash, encryption, transaction recording, access management in order to ensure the protection of personal data. If it is learned that personal data is obtained by others illegally, the situation will be immediately reported to the Personal Data Protection Board in accordance with the legal regulation and in writing.


Keeping Personal Data Current and Accurate

In accordance with Article 4 of the KVKK, Enmot Industry has the obligation to keep your personal data accurate and up-to-date. In this context, our customers are required to share their correct and up-to-date data or update them via the website/mobile application in order for Enmot Industry is able to fulfill its obligations arising from the current legislation.
