Our Goals & Principles

Enmot Industry aims to develop healthy together with its employees and offer products and services at universal quality and standards. It aims to be a symbol of trust, stability and respect for its country, customers, business partners, suppliers and the industrial sector.


Effective Customer Relations

To create added-value for our customers and to respond with quality and stability pursuant to their needs and demands are our priorities.


Being "the best" at all times is our indispensable goal.

Our main goal is to be the best in quality, service and 3rd party relations and to preserve this image we possess in public. In order to achieve this goal, it is our basic principle to undertake management and to be a leader in the market in the fields of activity.


Our most important saving is our human resources.

Our total quality management starts with the quality of our employees. To attract and employ the best personnel and adult labor force in our company, to benefit from the talents, force and creativity of our people, to increase their productivity, to enable them to develop, to create a working environment where cooperation and solidarity flourish are the approaches we have chosen to ensure the continuity of our organization for generations.


Our goal is to create resources for sustainable development.

To be able to make the necessary investments in order to provide sustainable service; to provide the shareholders with the profit that is entitled to the capital, in order to encourage the merger of small and big savings; to create resources from activities in order to help the economic and social development of our employees and the society and not to compromise wastefulness by ensuring the rational use of all resources are among our main principles.
